Public sector asset valuations, faster and audit-ready

Faster, more accurate, fully compliant valuations across complex public sector estates — combining RICS Registered Valuer oversight with proprietary tools built in-house to deliver programmes that are efficient, transparent, and audit-ready.

The requirement

Annual reporting is non-negotiable

Public sector organisations are under increasing pressure to maintain accurate asset records, support robust financial reporting, and make informed decisions across complex property portfolios.

Traditional valuation approaches can be time-consuming, resource-intensive, and difficult to scale. Assets are grouped by type and valued using the most appropriate method — most commonly replacement cost, estimating what it would cost to rebuild the asset today using a Modern Equivalent Alternative.
